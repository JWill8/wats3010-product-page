Welcome to Cascade Farms!

Who We Are

Cascade Farms is a third generation, family run Christmas Tree Farm located in the Pacific Northwest, we sell Christmas trees and custom Christmas wreaths, made right here on the farm. Let us help you make your holidays special!

About our Wreaths

Our Wreaths are hand made by exprienced wreath-makers to ensure every wreath is up to our quality standards. We use only Noble Fir branches, the most popular and preferred tree type for Christmas Trees, to make our wreaths
